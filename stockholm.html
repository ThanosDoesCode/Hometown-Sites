Visit Stockholm

Enjoy culture and food in Stockholm, the city of vikings.

Top three activities to do in Stockholm

Vasa Museum

World-famous Vasa ship, a 17th-century warship that sank on its maiden voyage and is now beautifully preserved.

ABBA The Museum

Dive into Sweden’s pop history at this interactive museum dedicated to ABBA. Sing, dance, and try on virtual costumes.

Gamla Stan (Old Town)

Photography museum featuring contemporary exhibitions and a top-floor café with breathtaking city views.

Top three foods to try in Athens

Swedish Meatballs (Köttbullar)

Served with creamy gravy, lingonberry jam, and mashed potatoes.

Cinnamon Buns (Kanelbullar)

Perfect for fika (Swedish coffee break), these spiced buns are a must-try

Herring (Sill)

pickled herring varieties, a staple in Swedish cuisine.

Top three places to visit in Athens

Skansen Open-Air Museum

A glimpse into Sweden’s past with traditional houses, Nordic animals, and seasonal markets.

Drottningholm Palace

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the Swedish royal family's residence, surrounded by beautiful gardens.

Fotografiska Museum

A photography museum featuring contemporary exhibitions and a top-floor café with breathtaking city views.
