Visit Athens

Enjoy culture and food in this historical city in the center of Ancient Greece.

Top three activities to do in Athens

Acropolis & Parthenon

Explore Athens’ iconic hilltop citadel and its ancient temple, the Parthenon

Plaka & Anafiotika

Stroll picturesque streets, Cycladic houses, and charming shops below the Acropolis.

Syntagma Square

Watch traditional Evzones perform their ceremonial guard-changing in front of Parliament.

Top three foods to try in Athens

Souvlaki/Gyro

Marinated grilled meat, fresh veggies, tzatziki, and fries wrapped in soft pita bread.

Moussaka

Layers of eggplant, minced meat, and creamy béchamel, baked to golden perfection.

Greek Salad

Fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, olives, and feta cheese, drizzled with olive oil and oregano.

Top three places to visit in Athens

National Archaeological Museum

Discover ancient Greek artifacts, including statues, pottery, and the famous Mask of Agamemnon.

Temple of Hephaestus

A beautifully preserved ancient Greek temple dedicated to the god of craftsmanship.

Academy of Athens

A neoclassical building with grand columns, sculptures, and symbolic architecture.
